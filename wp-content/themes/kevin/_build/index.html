About Me

Hi There!

I am Kevin and I am a Designer & Developer from Massachusetts. I feel in love with HTML & CSS in high school and decided I wanted to do this for the rest of my life. If I were a little kid and was asked what I wanted to be when I grow up I would probably say Web Designer.

I then went on to study at the University of Massachusetts Dartmouth College of Visual and Performing Arts where I studied Graphic Design & Digital Media. While I was there I learned everything from the rules of typography to 3D Animation.
